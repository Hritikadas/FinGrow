## Quick Registration Steps

### Using Namecheap (Recommended for beginners)

1. **Go to Namecheap.com**
2. **Search for your domain** (e.g., "fingrow.app")
3. **Add to cart** if available
4. **Create account** and checkout
5. **Verify email** and complete registration

### Using Cloudflare (Best value)

1. **Create Cloudflare account** at cloudflare.com
2. **Go to Domain Registration**
3. **Search and select domain**
4. **Complete purchase** (at-cost pricing)
5. **Domain automatically added to Cloudflare DNS**

## After Registration

Once you have your domain, you'll need to:

1. **Access DNS settings** in your registrar
2. **Add Resend DNS records** (provided by Resend)
3. **Update FinGrow configuration**
4. **Test email delivery**
